## Why

Traders need to see at a glance where each ticker sits within its options-implied expected move range. A ticker near the top of its weekly EM is "extended" (risky for new longs), while one near the bottom is at potential support. Currently this data exists in `data/expected-moves.json` but has no visual representation.

## What Changes

1. **New page: `expected-moves.html`** — Dedicated EM dashboard with:
   - Stats summary row (avg EM%, highest/lowest EM ticker, buy-zone count, extended count)
   - Tier tabs (Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Quarterly)
   - Sortable table with all EM tickers showing: close, current price, change, ±$ EM, EM %, low/high bounds, position bar, risk badge, risk meter
   - Color-coded risk scale: green (bottom of range) → red (top/extended)
   - Alert tags for actionable levels: 🟢 AT SUPPORT, 💚 NEAR LOW, ⚡ BELOW EM, ⚠️ AT CEILING, 🔴 ABOVE EM
   - Row highlighting for buy-zone and extended tickers
   - Click-through to watchlist detail modal via `?ticker=` param

2. **Watchlist detail EM banner** — In `watchlist.html` `openDetail()` modal:
   - Visual H/M/L band with current price marker (gold needle)
   - Daily/Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly tier cards with ±$ and % ranges
   - Outside-band detection (ABOVE/BELOW labels)
   - Futures proxy label when applicable

3. **Nav integration** — Added "Expected Moves" to shared nav bar

4. **Watchlist default view** — Switched from Widget (TradingView embed, hijacks clicks) to Table view

## Data Source

- `data/expected-moves.json` generated by `scripts/update-expected-moves-ib.py` (IB Gateway) or `scripts/update-expected-moves.py` (Polygon.io backup)
- ATM straddle × 0.85 method for weekly expected move
- 8 daily tier tickers: SPY, QQQ, DIA, IWM, IBIT, USO, UNG, GLD
- Weekly/Monthly/Quarterly tiers planned for full 70-ticker watchlist

## Risk Model

Position in range = `(currentPrice - lower) / (upper - lower) × 100`

| Position | Risk Level | Color | Long Signal |
|----------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| ≤10% | AT SUPPORT | Dark green | Strong buy zone |
| 10-20% | NEAR LOW | Green | Buy zone |
| 20-40% | LOW | Light green | Favorable |
| 40-55% | MODERATE | Yellow-green | Neutral |
| 55-70% | ELEVATED | Yellow | Caution |
| 70-85% | HIGH | Orange | Risky for new longs |
| 85-100% | EXTENDED | Red | Very risky |
| >100% | ABOVE EM | Dark red | Overextended |
| <0% | BELOW EM | Bright green | Potential bounce |
